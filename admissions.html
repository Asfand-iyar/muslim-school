💰 Fee Rules

  • 1. Fee must be paid before the 5th of each month, otherwise late fine of Rs. 50/- per day will be charged.
  • 2. Transfer certificate will NOT be issued until all outstanding dues are cleared.
  • 3. No student is allowed to sit for any examination unless outstanding dues are fully paid.
  • 4. Fee, once paid, including that paid in advance, is NOT refundable.
  • 5. Additional amount of Rs. 300/- is charged for Computer Science as a subject.

⏰ Late Fee Policy

Students who fail to pay their fee on time are fined as follows:

  • a. Fee must be paid by the 10th of every month, after which late fine of Rs. 50/- per day will be charged till 15th.
  • b. After 15th, student's name will be struck off from the school rolls.
