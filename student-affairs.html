📚 Student Affairs

Your complete guide to student policies, rules, and regulations at THE MUSLIM SCHOOL.

📋 Attendance Rules

100% attendance is essential for character building and excellence in academics.

  • 1. Attendance recorded twice daily (after morning assembly and after tea break).
  • 2. Latecomers will be sent back home.
  • 3. Leave must be applied in writing, signed by parent/guardian.
  • 4. Medical leave requires doctor's prescription and certificate.
  • 5. Absence for 3 consecutive days without valid reason = name struck off.
  • 6. Fine for absence without leave: Rs. 100/- to Rs. 2000/- per day.

📊 Results & Promotion Policy

  • • Pass mark in all internal examinations: 50%
  • • For promotion to next class: Must pass all subjects
  • • For Board classes (9th, 10th): Minimum 70% marks required in Send-up exams
  • • Result cards are issued after every examination
  • • Principal's decision on examination admissions is final

👪 Parent Teacher Meeting (PTM)

  • • PTM is held after every monthly test and terminal examination.
  • • Parents are required to attend PTM regularly to discuss their child's progress.
  • • In case of absence without prior intimation, fine of Rs. 500/- will be charged.
  • • PTM schedule is announced on the school notice board and website.

⚖️ General & Disciplinary Rules

The following offenses make a student liable for disciplinary action:

  • • Disobedience or disrespect towards staff members
  • • Using unfair means in examinations
  • • Use of abusive language or indecent behavior
  • • Theft or willful damage to school property
  • • Possession of mobile phones, weapons, or narcotics
  • • Physical violence or attempt thereof
  • • Habitual absenteeism

Note: The Principal's decision in all academic/disciplinary matters will be final and binding.

🚪 Withdrawal / Discharge Rules

  • • A written request for withdrawal must reach the office 1 month in advance.
  • • School Leaving Certificate will be issued only after all dues are cleared.
  • • Fee is not refundable in case of withdrawal mid-term.
  • • Students with repeated academic failure may be asked to leave.
  • • Serious disciplinary violations may lead to immediate expulsion.
