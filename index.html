Welcome to THE MUSLIM SCHOOL, Nowshera City – a rising educational network striving for excellence.

We prepare our students for 21st-century challenges with established international standards. Founded in 1995 with a mission to equip the coming generations with a curriculum to compete with the contemporary world. We believe every student is skillful and has unique qualities. Our teaching is based on identifying the nature of subjects and developing personal skills.

  • Birth Certificate (for Playgroup & Nursery)
  • 2 Passport size photographs
  • Parent/Guardian CNIC copy

🎓 Programs Offered

  • Prep (Prep I & Prep II)
  • Primary (1st – 5th Class)
  • Middle (6th – 8th Class)
  • High (9th – 10th Class – Matric)
