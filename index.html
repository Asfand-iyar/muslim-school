Admission requirements

Here you can find an initial overview of the general requirements and additional requirements for certain degree programs if you want to be admitted to our Bachelor’s and Master’s programs.

📘 General requirements

  • Previous School Certificate
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 (or country equivalent)
  • English proficiency (TOEFL / IELTS)
  • Letter of motivation

🎓 Bachelor’s programs

  • Specific subject prerequisites (Math/Science for Engineering)
  • Entrance exam (for competitive programs)
  • Personal interview (for Education & Arts)

📖 Master’s programs

  • Bachelor’s degree in related field
  • Minimum 2 years of relevant experience (for MBA)
  • Research proposal (for thesis-based programs)
  • Two recommendation letters

❓ You’re not sure whether your educational degree entitles you to study?

Please contact the staff at the Central Student Advising Service. They will guide you through equivalency and qualifications.
